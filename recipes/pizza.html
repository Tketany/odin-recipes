Pizza Recipe

Description

Pepperoni pizza is a classic and universally beloved dish that tantalizes the taste buds with its delicious combination of flavors and textures. At its core, this pizza features a thin or thick crust, depending on regional variations, that serves as the perfect canvas for a symphony of mouthwatering ingredients. The star of the show is the generous layer of thinly sliced pepperoni, a cured and seasoned Italian-American sausage, which delivers a robust and slightly spicy kick. Beneath the savory pepperoni, a rich tomato sauce is spread, infusing each bite with the vibrant essence of ripe tomatoes and a harmonious blend of herbs and spices. A blanket of melted mozzarella cheese crowns the pizza, offering a gooey, stretchy texture and a creamy, slightly tangy flavor that complements the other components. The interplay of these elements creates a delectable experience that satisfies the palate with a perfect balance of savory, tangy, and salty notes. Whether enjoyed as a quick snack, shared at a gathering, or savored as a comforting meal, pepperoni pizza has become a timeless favorite, capturing the essence of indulgence and culinary satisfaction.

Ingredients

Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 1/2 to 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Pizza Sauce:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Toppings:

  • Pepperoni slices (as much as desired)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Optional Toppings:

  • Sliced black olives
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Crushed red pepper flakes

Steps:

Pizza Dough:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Mix the ingredients together until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, or until it doubles in size.

Pizza Sauce:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the crushed tomatoes, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Simmer the sauce over low heat for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Adjust the seasoning to taste.

Assembling and Baking:

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature recommended for your pizza dough.
  2. Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, and arrange pepperoni slices (and any additional toppings) on top.
  6. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and enjoy your homemade pepperoni pizza!
