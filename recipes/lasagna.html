Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic and delectable Italian dish that embodies layers of culinary perfection. This savory and comforting masterpiece is constructed with wide sheets of pasta, meticulously layered with a rich and hearty combination of ingredients. The foundation typically consists of alternating strata of pasta sheets, velvety ricotta cheese, robust marinara sauce, and perfectly seasoned ground meat, often a flavorful blend of beef and pork. Each layer contributes its distinct texture and taste, creating a harmonious symphony of flavors that meld together during the baking process. A generous topping of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese crowns the dish, forming a golden, bubbly crust that adds a delightful crunch. Whether served as a centerpiece for family gatherings or intimate dinners, lasagna stands as a timeless embodiment of culinary craftsmanship, capable of satisfying the most discerning palates with its rich, savory, and utterly satisfying profile

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (about 9-12 sheets)
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 pound (225g) ground pork (or you can use all beef)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the Noodles:Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and ground pork over medium heat until fully cooked.
    • Add chopped onions and minced garlic. Sauté until the onions are softened.
    • Pour in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste. Stir well.
    • If using, add red wine for extra flavor.
    • Season with dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  4. Prepare the Cheese Mixture: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with a beaten egg. Mix well.
  5. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
    • Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Add a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture, spreading it evenly.
    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the ricotta layer.
    • Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous amount of mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  6. Bake:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the edges are golden brown.
  7. Let it Rest: Allow the lasagna to rest for about 10 minutes before serving. This helps to set the layers and makes it easier to slice.
  8. Garnish and Serve:Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.Slice and serve the lasagna, enjoying the layers of delicious flavors.
